What can we do for you?

It is often the case that on some flights there are empty seats left either due to cancellation or late arrivals by the customer, or not enough seats were sold to fill the plane, which is why airlines currently sell more tickets than seats on the plane, seeking to avoid this situation, even so, is based on predictive models that are not yet perfected and there are cases where there are still empty seats.

Our approach
Current solutions such as despegar.com or skyscanner.com have functions in the concept of "last minute flights" whose objective is to fill the plane in some way when it is ready to take off, however, has the disadvantage that being focused on flights in general and lose the focus of these last minute flights, which tends to confuse the user by the lack of clear exposure of information.
