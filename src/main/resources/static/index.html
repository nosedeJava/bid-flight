What can you do in this plattform?

Our application seeks to provide this service of "last minute flights" with the particularity that airlines have the opportunity to auction these empty seats, so that users have the opportunity to access these flights, the goal is that these auctions start with a low bid to make it attractive to the user.

Our objective
Our application seeks that in high seasons as there is a high flow of users looking for flights the bids will rise and generate benefits to the airlines while giving users the opportunity to get the flights they want, however not only benefits the airlines in financial terms as in low seasons the bids are expected to remain low and users can get the flights cheaper than usual.
